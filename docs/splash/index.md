# Welcome

Welcome to the Splash documentation.

Splash turns Foundry journal pages into interactive fullscreen handouts — splash screens that cover the canvas, clickable puzzles and combination locks, table-wide votes, and branching story screens. You build them in a visual editor and show them to your players from the scene controls, from a journal page, or from a trigger like a player clicking a locked door.

## Foundry compatibility

- **Minimum**: 13.344
- **Maximum**: 14

Splash requires [lib-wrapper](https://github.com/ruipin/fvtt-lib-wrapper).
